Paper Format


WAAMI 2021 workshop papers will be published by IEEE in the main conference proceedings. Papers will be selected by a single blind (reviewers are anonymous) review process based on their originality, relevance, and clarity of presentation.


Submission must be formatted in accordance with ICCV guidelines given at http://iccv2021.thecvf.com/node/4#submission-guidelines. You should use the same template as ICCV, replacing 'ICCV paper' with 'ICCV workshop paper'. Submissions should be 4 to 8 pages excluding references.


Once accepted, at least one author is expected to attend the event and present the paper. Accepted papers will be presented as Oral or Poster presentations during the workshop.
